# Popular Zombie Packs And Maps

This file captures the research snapshot used for the April 17, 2026 preset pass.
The "most popular" ordering here is an inference from visible workshop download totals plus reuse suitability, not an official Bohemia popularity ranking.

## Selection approach

The goal here was not "every zombie-related mod on the workshop." The goal was:

- public-use packs that people can actually build on,
- terrain choices that show real zombie-scenario gravity,
- enough signal to stop the director defaults from feeling generic.

## Top pack set used for presets

### 1. Bacon Zombies

- Role: core actor pack
- Why it matters: actual zombies and zombie groups, with multiple special variants
- Workshop traction: 475,717 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/622120A5448725E3-BaconZombies>

### 2. ToH Zombie

- Role: cosmetic infected heads/faces layer
- Why it matters: strong traction and clean public license, works well as a visual overlay on top of Bacon-backed scenarios
- Workshop traction: 31,384 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/669D29B6A22342D0>

### 3. ARMST PLATFORM - Mutants

- Role: special-event mutant pack
- Why it matters: highest-signal public mutant add-on I found that is still reusable, good for labs and contamination zones
- Workshop traction: 23,284 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/6316DCFBDF1A346F-ARMSTPLATFORM-Mutants>

### 4. WCS_Zombies

- Role: WCS compatibility layer
- Why it matters: widely used enough to justify preset support on WCS-oriented servers
- Workshop traction: 12,435 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/6288865B735F0F0E>

### 5. Zombie Characters

- Role: elite flavor variants
- Why it matters: smaller than the others, but still the most useful public flavor pack I found for scenario authors who want more visual variety
- Workshop traction: 3,697 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/66C311AE11A083C8>

## Popular but excluded pack

### ReforgedZ_Zombies

- Workshop traction: 25,107 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/669D67EB93BF0F75>
- Why excluded: the workshop page says the content may only be used on authorised ReforgedZ servers and unauthorised hosting, deployment, modification, or distribution is prohibited.

That makes it a bad fit for a reusable "anyone can use this" public director preset set.

## Map set used for presets

### 1. Everon

- Source: <https://community.bohemia.net/wiki/Arma_Reforger:Everon>
- Why included: base-game anchor map, broadest terrain mix, strong fit for long-form hunt pacing

### 2. Arland

- Source: <https://community.bohemia.net/wiki/Arma_Reforger:Arland>
- Why included: base-game compact map, good fit for intimate pacing and direct reinforcement behavior

### 3. Dead Everon

- Workshop traction: 178,158 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/5D58A217A9611AFB>
- Why included: by far the most important zombie-heavy workshop terrain in the source set

### 4. Dead Everon 2

- Workshop traction: 11,743 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/68851B84C4FB36A1>
- Why included: strongest newer successor terrain with active zombie/STALKER usage

### 5. Woodbury

- Workshop traction: 11,629 downloads
- Source: <https://reforger.armaplatform.com/workshop/60CC1A8665DEB4F3>
- Why included: compact North American map with strong zombie-PvE applicability and natural choke-point play
